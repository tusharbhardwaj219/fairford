1. Introduction

Fair Ford Pharmaceuticals operates as a premium B2B pharmaceutical marketplace connecting verified distributors, stockists, retailers, and healthcare businesses for medicine procurement and supply-chain management across India.

2. Acceptance of Terms

By accessing this platform, users agree to comply with all Terms & Conditions, pharmaceutical regulations, healthcare compliance standards, and Indian laws governing pharmaceutical distribution.

3. User Eligibility

  • Only verified pharmacies and distributors may register.
  • Drug License and GST verification are mandatory.
  • Fake registrations may result in permanent suspension.
  • Healthcare businesses must provide valid documentation.

4. Registration & Verification

Users are required to complete KYC verification, license verification, and administrator approval before accessing pricing and ordering systems.

5. Product Information

  • Product availability may change without notice.
  • Medicine images are for reference purposes only.
  • Pricing is visible only after account approval.
  • Some medicines may require prescription authorization.

6. Orders & Payments

Orders are subject to inventory availability and approval. Fair Ford Pharmaceuticals reserves the right to reject suspicious transactions or fraudulent orders.

7. Delivery & Logistics

Delivery timelines may vary depending on regional territory allocations and distributor availability. Delays caused by logistics or regulatory issues are not guaranteed liabilities.

8. Territory Allocation Policy

Distributors may receive territory-based authorization. Unauthorized cross-region selling may lead to account suspension or termination.

9. Pharmaceutical Compliance

  • Indian pharmaceutical regulations
  • GST compliance laws
  • Drug safety standards
  • Healthcare distribution guidelines

10. Prohibited Activities

  • Fake licenses
  • Unauthorized medicine resale
  • Counterfeit medicines
  • Data theft
  • System abuse or hacking attempts

11. Privacy & Data Security

User information and uploaded documents are stored using secure encrypted systems. Limited third-party sharing applies only for compliance and operational purposes.

12. Limitation of Liability

Fair Ford Pharmaceuticals shall not be liable for indirect damages, delivery delays, technical downtime, or third-party distributor activities.

13. Account Suspension & Termination

Accounts may be suspended for fraud, fake documentation, illegal activity, policy violations, or regulatory non-compliance.

14. Updates to Terms

Fair Ford Pharmaceuticals reserves the right to modify Terms & Conditions without prior notice. Continued use of the platform indicates acceptance of updated terms.
